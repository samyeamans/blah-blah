About GeoWorlder

The story behind the ultimate geography guessing game

Our Mission

GeoWorlder was created with a simple mission: to make learning geography fun, engaging, and free for everyone. We believe that understanding our world's geography is not just about memorizing capitals and borders, but about appreciating the diversity and beauty of our planet.

How It Started

The idea for GeoWorlder came in early 2025 when our founder wanted to create a way for people to virtually explore places they might never have the chance to visit in person. What started as a small project quickly grew into the interactive and competitive game you see today.

How It Works

GeoWorlder uses Google Street View to transport players to random locations around the world. Your challenge? Figure out where you are based on visual clues, then make your best guess on the map. The closer your guess, the higher your score!
