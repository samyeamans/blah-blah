1. Introduction

This Cookies Policy explains how GeoWorlder ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website, mobile application, and services (collectively, the "Service"). This policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy, which explains how we use your personal information.

By continuing to use our Service, you are agreeing to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Cookies allow websites to recognize your device and preferences, remember the items in your shopping cart, and analyze how you use the website to improve your experience.

In addition to cookies, we may also use similar technologies such as:

  • Web beacons: Small graphic images that allow us to monitor the use of our websites.
  • Pixel tags: Tiny transparent images that are embedded in emails to tell us when you've opened them.
  • Local storage: A way for websites to store information on your device similar to cookies but with larger storage capacity.

5. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to manage your cookie preferences. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent. The methods for doing so vary from browser to browser, and from version to version.

You can generally find information about how to manage cookies in the "Help" section of your browser, or in the settings.

Please note that if you choose to block or delete cookies, certain features of our Service may not operate correctly or at all.

You can also opt out of some third-party cookies directly through the relevant third-party:

  • Google Analytics: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
  • Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/settings/?tab=ads

You can also visit the following websites to learn more about cookies and how you can manage them:

  • AboutCookies.org
  • Your Online Choices (EU)
  • Network Advertising Initiative (US)
